About Me

Matt was born in Varginha, Brazil, but he has spent 18 years of his life living here in Utah. From a young age, Matt has been passionate about video games, computers, and potential technologies of the future. He is inspired by the field of computer programming because he believes it is the only profession where individuals are completely in control of their personal successes. His goal is to eventually own and operate a software company specializing in various media services, with an emphasis on gaming.
